The Cumberland is one of the East Cliff's most recognisable buildings a handsome 1930s art-deco hotel whose curved white lines and sun-trap terrace capture the golden age of the British seaside. Overlooking Poole Bay, it makes an atmospheric base for a break by the coast.

## The stay

Rooms range from comfortable doubles to sea-facing options that look out over the bay, with doubles from around £110. The real showpiece is the outdoor swimming pool and terrace, while the restaurant and bar mean you don't have to venture far for dinner or a drink. Families are well catered for.

## The location

The hotel sits along the East Cliff, a short, level walk from the clifftop gardens and the lift down to the beach, and only a few minutes from the town centre. For an eco-conscious alternative nearby, see [The Green House Hotel](/listing/the-green-house-hotel), or for a self-catering stay consider [Tower House Apartments](/listing/tower-house-apartments).

## Good to know

On-site parking is available, which is a real bonus on the cliff. Book direct and ask about sea-view rooms and pool access when you reserve.

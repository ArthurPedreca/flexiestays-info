Chines Coffee House is a warm, independent café tucked into Westbourne's characterful arcade quarter. It's the sort of neighbourhood place where regulars drop in for a flat white and a chat, and where the focus is firmly on good coffee, unfussy brunch and a genuinely relaxed welcome.

## Coffee and food

The team takes its coffee seriously, with speciality beans and proper barista drinks, backed up by an all-day brunch menu, homemade cakes and light lunches. There are vegetarian choices throughout, and prices are gentle, starting from around £3 for a coffee.

## The setting

You'll find it among the independent shops and boutiques of Westbourne village, only a few doors from [Koh Thai Tapas](/listing/koh-thai-tapas). It's dog-friendly and family-friendly, with free Wi-Fi, so it works just as well for a quiet catch-up as for a pit stop.

## Good to know

It's a natural base for exploring Westbourne on foot, and an easy walk down to the coast at Alum Chine. Tables can fill up at weekend brunch time, so arrive a little earlier if you want a relaxed sit-down.

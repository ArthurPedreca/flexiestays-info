Tower House Apartments offer bright, self-contained accommodation on the West Cliff, ideal if you like the freedom of self-catering with the beach and town both close at hand. Each apartment comes with its own kitchen and living space, making them a flexible choice for families, couples and longer stays.

## The apartments

Expect comfortable, practical spaces with everything you need to settle in an equipped kitchen, free Wi-Fi and self check-in for a fuss-free arrival. Nightly rates start from around £120, and family-sized options mean there's room to spread out. Please note the apartments are not dog-friendly.

## The location

The West Cliff position puts you within easy reach of the seafront, with the zig-zag paths and lift down to the sand, and a short, level walk into the town centre for shops and restaurants. On-site parking makes arriving by car straightforward.

## Good to know

If you'd prefer a full-service stay nearby, compare with [The Cumberland Hotel](/listing/the-cumberland-hotel) on the East Cliff or the eco-friendly [The Green House Hotel](/listing/the-green-house-hotel). Book ahead for peak summer weeks, when West Cliff self-catering fills quickly.

The Green House is Bournemouth's best-known eco hotel a beautifully restored Victorian villa on the East Cliff where sustainability runs through everything, from the locally sourced kitchen to the low-impact fit-out, without sacrificing comfort.

## The stay

Rooms are individually styled and calm, and the restaurant leans on Dorset producers and seasonal menus. It's an easy walk to the clifftop, the beach lift and the town centre, making it a relaxed base for a longer break.

## Good to know

Book direct for the best rates, and ask about sea-view and pet-friendly rooms when you reserve. Parking and EV charging are available on site.

The Oceanarium sits right on the seafront at the foot of the pier, and is Bournemouth's aquarium a compact indoor attraction that takes you on a journey through the world's waters without leaving the coast. It's a reliable favourite for families and an easy answer to a rainy Dorset afternoon.

## What you'll see

Themed habitats lead you from the local waters of **Poole Bay** out to the **Amazon** rainforest, the icy **Antarctic** and the vivid coral of the **Great Barrier Reef**. A walk-through tunnel puts you eye-to-eye with the residents, and a full timetable of daily talks and feeding sessions helps bring each zone to life.

## Visiting

The Oceanarium is step-free throughout, with a café, gift shop and baby-changing facilities. Allow one to two hours. Tickets start from around £13, and booking online ahead of your visit usually saves both money and time in the summer queues.

## Nearby

You're a moment from [Bournemouth Pier](/listing/bournemouth-pier) and the beach, with the [Lower Gardens](/listing/lower-gardens) stretching back up towards the town centre.

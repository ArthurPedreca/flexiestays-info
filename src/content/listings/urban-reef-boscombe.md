Urban Reef is a relaxed, all-day spot on the Boscombe overcliff, perched above the promenade with wide views across the bay. It's equally happy as a morning coffee stop, a long weekend brunch or a laid-back dinner, and its easy-going feel makes it a favourite with locals and visitors alike.

## The food

The kitchen champions Dorset producers and runs its own bakery, so expect fresh bread and pastries alongside a menu that shifts from brunch plates to seasonal mains as the day goes on. Prices are approachable, with dishes from around £12, and there's a decent choice for children and dog owners too.

## The setting

The big draw is the position right on the clifftop above [Boscombe Beach](/listing/boscombe-beach), a short walk from [Boscombe Pier](/listing/boscombe-pier). Grab a table on the terrace on a clear day and you'll see right across Poole Bay.

## Good to know

It gets busy at weekends and in summer, so it's worth booking for brunch or dinner. The overcliff location is a gentle stroll from the beach lift and the pier approach.

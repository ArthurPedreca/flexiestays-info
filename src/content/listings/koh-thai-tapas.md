Koh Thai Tapas brings a fun, sharing-style approach to Thai food right in the middle of Westbourne. Rather than one plate each, the idea is to order a spread of small dishes for the table, so everyone gets to sample the fragrant curries, stir-fries and street-food snacks the kitchen turns out.

## The food and drink

Plates start at around £7 and are designed to be shared, which makes it easy to build a meal around whatever you fancy. There's plenty for vegetarians and vegans, and the cocktail list is a genuine draw this is a place that fills up with a buzzy, evening-out atmosphere.

## Where it is

Koh Thai sits among the independent shops and cafés of Westbourne village, an easy walk from [Chines Coffee House](/listing/chines-coffee-house) if you're making a day of the area. The village is a short hop from Bournemouth town centre and the seafront at Alum Chine.

## Good to know

It's popular with groups and gets busy at weekends, so book ahead for larger tables or a Friday or Saturday evening.

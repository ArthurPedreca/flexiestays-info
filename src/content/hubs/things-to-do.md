Bournemouth packs a full holiday into a compact, walkable town. In a single day you can swim off a sandy beach, ride a Victorian pier, wander landscaped gardens and watch the sun set over Poole Bay from a clifftop.

This is the complete list of things to do the headline attractions, the free gardens and green spaces, the watersports on Boscombe seafront, and the coastal walks that lead out to nature reserves at either end of the bay. Filter by area to plan around where you're staying.

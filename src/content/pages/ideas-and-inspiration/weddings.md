With its golden sands, subtropical gardens and dramatic clifftops, Bournemouth is one of the south coast's most romantic places to marry. Whether you dream of bare feet on the beach or a grand seafront hotel, the town has a setting to suit.

## Beach and seafront ceremonies

Few backdrops beat the sea. Several stretches of Bournemouth's coast can host beach ceremonies and celebrations, with the sand, the pier and the sweep of Poole Bay as your backdrop. It is an unforgettable, relaxed way to say "I do".

## Clifftop and garden venues

For sweeping views, the clifftops above the town offer elegant hotels and function rooms overlooking the bay. Inland, the town's historic gardens and country houses provide leafy, sheltered settings for photographs and outdoor drinks receptions.

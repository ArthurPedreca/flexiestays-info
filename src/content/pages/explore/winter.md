Winter shows a different, more atmospheric side of Bournemouth. The town sparkles with festive lights, the beaches turn wild and beautiful, and its mild coastal climate keeps the worst of the weather at bay.

## Christmas Tree Wonderland

The highlight of the season is the Christmas Tree Wonderland, when the town centre and gardens are transformed by a glittering trail of illuminated trees and light displays. It is a magical, free way to spend a December evening, and a firm favourite with families.

## Festive markets and shopping

Seasonal markets bring stalls of crafts, gifts and warming food and drink to the town, while the Victorian arcades of Westbourne and the independent shops of Boscombe make for characterful festive browsing away from the chain stores.

## Bracing beach walks

Winter is the time for invigorating walks along near-empty sands, followed by a warming coffee or lunch in a cosy seafront café. The clifftops are dramatic under moody skies, and the sunsets over Poole Bay are among the best of the year.

Planning a visit to Bournemouth? This is your starting point. We have gathered the practical essentials when to come, how to get here and what the weather has in store so you can spend less time organising and more time enjoying the coast.

## When to go

Bournemouth is a year-round destination, but the right time depends on what you are after buzzing summer beaches or quiet off-season walks. See our guide to [when to visit Bournemouth](/visitor-information/when-to-go) to pick your season.

## Getting here

Whether you are coming by train, car, coach or plane, Bournemouth is easy to reach from London and the rest of the UK. Our [travel information](/visitor-information/travel-information) page covers routes, journey times and parking.

## Weather and climate

The town enjoys one of the sunniest, mildest microclimates in Britain. Find out what to expect and what to pack in our guide to [Bournemouth's weather and climate](/visitor-information/weather).

## What's on and where to stay

Once you have the basics covered, see [what's on](/whats-on) during your dates and browse places to stay in our [accommodation](/accommodation) listings. From there, dive into [things to do](/things-to-do) to build your itinerary.

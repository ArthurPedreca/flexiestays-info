The Bournemouth Guide respects your privacy. This policy explains, in plain English, what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information we collect

We collect very little. If you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in your message. We use this only to reply to you and to act on your enquiry or correction. We do not sell or share it.

## Cookies and analytics

Like most websites, we may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which pages are popular and how the site is performing. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. Where cookies are used, your browser lets you block or delete them at any time.

## Third-party links

Our pages link to other websites official tourism sites, venues and booking providers. Once you follow a link, you are subject to that site's own privacy policy, not ours. We are not responsible for how third parties handle your data.

## Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you, request a copy, or ask us to delete it. Because we hold so little, this is usually straightforward.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about your privacy or this policy, please [contact us](/contact) at hello@thebournemouthguide.com.

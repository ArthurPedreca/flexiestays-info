We love hearing from readers, local businesses and anyone who cares about getting Bournemouth right. Whether you have a question, a suggestion or a correction, we read every message.

## Email us

The quickest way to reach us is by email at [hello@thebournemouthguide.com](mailto:hello@thebournemouthguide.com). We aim to reply within a few working days.

## List your business

Run a hotel, restaurant, café, shop or attraction in Bournemouth, Boscombe, Southbourne, Westbourne or the surrounding area? We are always expanding our listings. Send us a short description of your business, your address and a link to your website, and we will be in touch about featuring you across the guide.

## Spotted something out of date?

We work hard to keep every page accurate, but opening times change, cafés move and prices creep up. If you notice something that no longer looks right a closed venue, a wrong detail, a broken link please tell us. Include the page and what needs fixing, and we will update it promptly.

## Editorial and partnerships

For press enquiries, content partnerships or advertising, drop us a line at the same address with "Partnerships" in the subject line.

## Before you write

Many common questions are already answered across the site. You might find what you need in our [visitor information](/visitor-information), [things to do](/things-to-do) or [accommodation](/accommodation) sections. Not there? We would still love to hear from you.

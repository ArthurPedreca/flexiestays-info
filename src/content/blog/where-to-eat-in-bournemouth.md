Bournemouth's dining scene has grown up in recent years, with a strong showing of seafront restaurants, independent cafés and global flavours. Here are four places we return to again and again.

## West Beach seafood with a sea view

Perched right on the sand near the pier, [West Beach](/listing/west-beach-restaurant) is the town's go-to for fresh Dorset seafood. Book a terrace table at sunset for the full seafront experience.

## Urban Reef Boscombe brunch and dinner

Overlooking Boscombe Pier, [Urban Reef](/listing/urban-reef-boscombe) is a relaxed all-day spot serving local produce, from weekend brunch to sundowner cocktails as the surfers head in.

## Koh Thai Tapas small plates, big flavours

For something livelier, [Koh Thai Tapas](/listing/koh-thai-tapas) reimagines Thai cooking as sharing plates, with a buzzy atmosphere that suits groups and date nights alike.

## Chines Coffee House a proper local café

When you want quality coffee, cake and a friendly welcome, [Chines Coffee House](/listing/chines-coffee-house) is an independent favourite that captures Bournemouth's easy-going café culture.

## Make a day of it

Pair lunch with a stroll along the [beaches](/things-to-do/beaches), or explore more options across our [food and drink](/food-and-drink) guide. Staying over? Find somewhere to rest in our [accommodation](/accommodation) listings.

Few UK seaside towns are as effortlessly family-friendly as Bournemouth. Gently shelving sand, plenty of shade and attractions within walking distance make it easy to fill a day without a car or a meltdown.

## Build sandcastles on Bournemouth Beach

Start on [Bournemouth Beach](/listing/bournemouth-beach), where the soft, clean sand and gently sloping water make paddling safe for little ones. Lifeguards patrol the busiest stretches in summer, and beach huts, toilets and ice-cream stops are never far away.

## Go under the sea at the Oceanarium

When you need a break from the sun, the [Oceanarium](/listing/oceanarium-bournemouth) is a winner. Children love the walk-through tunnel, the penguins and the daily feeding talks and under-fives go free, so it is easy on the budget too.

## Have some fun on the pier

[Bournemouth Pier](/listing/bournemouth-pier) is packed with family appeal: amusements, seafront strolling and, for braver kids, the thrilling zip wire back to the beach. The RockReef climbing centre at the pier entrance is another rainy-day saver.

## Tips for a smooth family day

Arrive early in peak season to grab a good spot, pack layers even on sunny days, and use the land train that runs along the promenade to save little legs. For more ideas, browse our family-friendly [things to do](/things-to-do) and see [what's on](/whats-on) during the school holidays.

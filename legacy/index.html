About Flexiestays

Flexiestays is a direct-booking platform and aparthotel operator in Bournemouth, on the south coast of England. It runs 130 serviced apartments across 7 buildings in Dorset, ranging from newly built seaside aparthotels to a restored Victorian property, all close to Bournemouth Beach and the town centre.

Unlike a marketplace, Flexiestays owns and manages its apartments and lets guests book directly through flexiestays.com. Because there is no third-party platform in the middle, guests skip the 14–20% service fees that Airbnb and Booking.com add so the same apartment usually costs less booked direct. As Flexiestays puts it, the team is "not just a booking platform" a local team is on hand 24/7 for support and local tips.

Every apartment comes with smart self check-in, fast Wi-Fi, smart TVs, fully equipped kitchens and comfortable beds, with free parking at most properties. Flexiestays suits short weekend breaks, longer holidays, remote work, relocations and corporate long-stays alike, and returning guests unlock extra savings through the FlexiePerks loyalty scheme.

Staying in Bournemouth

Flexiestays apartments sit on the Dorset coast in the centre of Bournemouth, a short walk from seven miles of golden, Blue Flag sandy beach and the Victorian pier. Most properties are clustered around the West Cliff, minutes from both the seafront and the town centre's restaurants, bars and shops Tower House, for example, sits roughly 450 yards from Alum Chine beach and 550 yards from Westcliff Beach.

The town is the gateway to the Jurassic Coast, England's only natural UNESCO World Heritage Site. Within easy reach you'll find the natural rock arch at Durdle Door, the near-perfect bay at Lulworth Cove, the sandy spit and harbour at Sandbanks and Poole, and the quiet heathland headland of Hengistbury Head. It's an ideal base whether you're here for a weekend by the sea, a longer family holiday, or an extended remote-work stay.

Bournemouth is also a year-round events town. Guests often book with Flexiestays for conferences and shows at the Bournemouth International Centre (BIC), summer beach festivals, and seasonal highlights along the seafront with a calm, self-contained apartment to come back to at the end of the day.

Frequently asked questions

What is Flexiestays?
Flexiestays is a direct-booking platform and operator of serviced apartments and aparthotels in Bournemouth, Dorset. It runs 130 apartments across 7 buildings and offers short, holiday and long-term stays all booked directly through flexiestays.com rather than through a third-party marketplace.
How is Flexiestays different from Airbnb or Booking.com?
Flexiestays owns and operates its own apartments and lets guests book direct, so there is no marketplace fee. Booking direct avoids the 14–20% service fees that Airbnb and Booking.com add, you deal with the local team rather than an intermediary, and you get access to the FlexiePerks loyalty scheme.
How does check-in work?
Every apartment uses smart self check-in. You receive your access details and directions before arrival, so you can let yourself in at your convenience without queuing at a reception desk.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Standard check-in is from 3:00pm and check-out is by 10:00am. Early check-in or late check-out can sometimes be arranged with the team, subject to availability, and your exact times are confirmed on your booking.
What's the payment process?
You can secure your stay by paying just 25% at the time of booking, with the balance due before arrival. Payment is handled securely through the Flexiestays platform.
What is your cancellation policy?
Flexiestays offers flexible cancellation options that vary by rate and length of stay. The full policy is set out on the Flexiestays help centre and is shown clearly before you confirm a booking.
Are the properties professionally cleaned?
Yes. Every apartment is professionally cleaned and prepared between stays to a consistent standard one of the most frequently praised points in guest reviews.
Do you offer parking?
Most Flexiestays properties include free on-site or private parking. Parking availability is listed on each individual apartment page so you can confirm before booking.
Do the apartments have Wi-Fi and Smart TVs?
Yes. All apartments come with fast Wi-Fi and smart TVs as standard, alongside fully equipped kitchens, comfortable beds and the essentials for both short breaks and longer stays.
Do you offer discounts for longer stays?
Yes. Long-term stays are offered at discounted weekly and monthly rates with bills included, making Flexiestays a practical option for relocations, remote work and extended business trips.
Are pets allowed?
Pet policies vary by apartment. If you're travelling with a pet, check the individual listing or contact the Flexiestays team before booking to confirm availability of a pet-friendly apartment.
What if I have an issue during my stay?
The Flexiestays local team is available 24/7 by phone, WhatsApp and email to help with anything during your stay, from practical questions to local recommendations.
Do you work with property owners?
Yes. Flexiestays partners with property owners in Bournemouth, managing and marketing their apartments through the platform. Owners can get in touch via the contact page to discuss working together.
